Become the Guardian Angel

Am kindly asking you to join the fundraising for defense systems against enemy drones “Become the Guardian Angel”.

In their free time, military personnel of Ukraine are engaged in developing and creating means of protection against enemy artillery and drones. Namely, they build and improve remote antenna and radio relay station systems. This allows them to stay in dugouts and shelters, during sniper fire, artillery salvos, and kamikaze drone attacks. The team includes many volunteers who go to the laboratory to construct and help after their day jobs. Engineers, electronic engineers, programmers, and circuit designers devote their free time to a good cause, thus protecting those who defend them on the front lines. We need funding to collect devices, update and repair already operating ones.

There is an urgent need for three complexes for the 57th brigade and Alfa (the SBU) for the nearest future. One entire complex is $4,000, without a drone it costs $2,000. We are confident in the victory of light over destruction and violence. And we have only one desire - to save the lives of the defenders. The price of victory depends on the actions of each of us. We are infinitely grateful to everyone for their help and contribution to our victory!
